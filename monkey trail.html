Monkey Trail

The great interest in the small transition Monkey trail is an accidental discovery during my Ao Nang play. Play guide Monkey trail is a somewhat steep trail, if you have bad legs, it is not necessary to walk. The stairs are short, but you can see the beautiful surroundings.
